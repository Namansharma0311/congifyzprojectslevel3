FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Cognifyz Internship Program

info

What is the Cognifyz Internship Program?

The Cognifyz IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Cognifyz Technologies) Internship Program is a structured learning experience designed to provide students and individuals with hands-on exposure to real-world projects in the fields of technology, software development, and related areas.

person_add

Who is eligible to apply?

The internship program is open to students and individuals with varying levels of experience and educational backgrounds. Specific eligibility criteria may be outlined in individual internship postings.

payments

Is the internship paid?

The internship program is unpaid for individuals who do not opt for the interview process. Those who choose to undergo the interview process will be subject to a nominal administrative cost. Successful candidates become eligible for the stipend.

app_registration

How can I apply?

To apply for the internship program, visit our internship portal. Follow the application instructions provided for each internship listing, and submit the required documents.

how_to_reg

How are interns selected?

Interns are selected based on their application, resume, and, in some cases, an interview. The selection process may vary for different internship positions.

schedule

What is the duration?

The duration of the internship is specified in the internship offer letter provided to selected candidates. It varies depending on the nature of the internship, typically ranging from 1 to 3 months.

workspace_premium

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, interns who successfully complete the internship program and fulfill any specified criteria will receive a certificate acknowledging their participation and achievements.

library_add_check

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you can apply for multiple internship positions if you meet the eligibility criteria for each position. Ensure that you submit separate applications for each role.
